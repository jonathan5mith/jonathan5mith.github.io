Kick is a completely new way for runners to race, and a fun way to stay motivated. You race against your friends, but you're ranked on how you've done against recent performances. So,

Challenge

You'll create a Kick and share with your Twitter followers who can join your race. A Kick could have just 2 runners or many more, it's up to you!

Run

You'll run your race, log however you want to (e.g. Parkrun, Strava, Garmin), and send your time to us here at Kick

Reward

You'll win points based on your results and collect badges (we call them vests) along the way.
